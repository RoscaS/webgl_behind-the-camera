Behind the camera (group B4)

HE-Arc, november 2019
Infography class: WebGL project
by Tristan Seuret, Nathan Latino and Sol Rosca

This project allows the user to visualize the camera's frustrum. By using a technique allowing us to split a view in differentes parts, two scenes using differents camera are created. The scene on the left is what the camera sees and the one on the right is another camera seeing the first camera and it's frustrum.

References: WebGL.info, tutorialspoint.com, webglfundamentals.org, riptutorial.com
